Symmetry Mate

Makes two similar entities be symmetric about a planar face or plane.

Example:

Download Example parts

  1. Select the parts as shown in the video given below from the downloaded file

  2. Duplicate the part and make sure to float the first part.

  3. Now click on the Mate button in the ribbon tab.

  4. Select the Symmetry mate inside the Advanced mate dropdown.
    Select the right plane under the plane selection prompter
    and select the faces as shown in the video under the face selection promptter.

  5. Press the OK button or check the green tick mark to apply the mate.

  6. Watch the video to learn more consicely.
