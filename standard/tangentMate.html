Tangent Mate

Places a cylindrical, spherical, or conical face tangent with another component.

Example:

Download Example parts

  1. Select the parts as shown in the video given below from the downloaded file

  2. select the roller type part and make sure to Float it by right clicking on the part and selecting float option
    Now make it coincident with the front plane

  3. Again right click on the roller part and make it fix, after that insert the another part as shown in the video

  4. Apply coincident mate to the selected faces as shown in the video

  5. Follow the video and apply tangent mate to the highlighted faces

  6. You will notice that the edge of the second part always remain in tangent to the circumference of the roller part.
